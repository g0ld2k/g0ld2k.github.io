At Zoner, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes what data Zoner stores, how it is used, and what it never does.

### **No Tracking or Analytics**

Zoner does not track your activity within the app and does not collect analytics. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or behavioural monitoring of any kind. Your usage patterns remain entirely private.

### **Data You Create**

All data in Zoner is created and controlled entirely by you. This includes:

- **Timezone locations** — cities, map coordinates, and IANA timezone identifiers you add to the app.
- **People** — names, avatar photos, and timezone associations you create for your teammates.
- **Groups** — team names and group memberships you define.
- **Working hours** — optional availability windows you set for locations or people.
- **Preferences** — settings such as time format and display options.

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party.

### **Avatar Photos**

If you assign avatar photos to people in Zoner, those images are stored on your device and, if iCloud sync is enabled, in your personal iCloud account. Photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

### **iCloud Sync**

Zoner uses iCloud and CloudKit to sync your data across your Apple devices. Data stored in iCloud is secured under your Apple ID and governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to data stored in your iCloud account.

### **App Group Storage**

On macOS, Zoner shares a small amount of data between the main app and the menu bar helper via a shared App Group container. This data stays entirely on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

### **Backup and Export**

Zoner allows you to export and import backup files. These files contain your Zoner data and are stored wherever you choose to save them (for example, Files or iCloud Drive). We have no access to backup files you create or import.

### **Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Zoner after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
