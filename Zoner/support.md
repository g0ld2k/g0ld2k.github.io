## Troubleshooting

### iCloud Sync

**Data isn't appearing on another device.**
Make sure both devices are signed into the same Apple ID and that iCloud is enabled for Zoner in **Settings → \[Your Name\] → iCloud**. Sync may take a few minutes when the app first launches or after a period offline.

**Duplicate items after sync.**
This can happen if the same item was created on two devices before they had a chance to sync. You can safely delete the duplicate — Zoner will not re-create it.

**Sync seems stuck.**
On iPhone, background and relaunch Zoner. On macOS, quit and reopen the app. If the issue persists, reach out at [zoner@g0ld2k.com](mailto:zoner@g0ld2k.com).

---

### Widgets

**The widget is showing stale data.**
Widgets update on a system-managed schedule. Opening the Zoner app triggers a refresh. If the widget stays stale, remove it from your Home Screen and re-add it.

**The widget is blank or showing an error.**
Remove the widget, relaunch Zoner, then add the widget again. Make sure you have at least one location or person saved before configuring a widget.

---

### macOS Menu Bar Helper

**The Zoner icon isn't appearing in the menu bar.**
Open Zoner, go to **Settings → Menu Bar**, and confirm that **Show in Menu Bar** is turned on. If the icon still doesn't appear, toggle the setting off and back on.

**There are two Zoner icons in the menu bar.**
This can occasionally happen after an update or system restart. Quit Zoner from the Dock and relaunch it — the app cleans up the duplicate automatically on startup.

**The menu bar helper launches at login but I didn't enable that.**
Turn off **Show in Menu Bar** in **Settings → Menu Bar**. The helper will no longer launch at login.
